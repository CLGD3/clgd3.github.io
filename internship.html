Internship Work

Internship Experience

Interning as the Career Services Graphic Designer has been one of the most important experiences I've had during my time at college. I have both learned and grown so much, and seeing the progression of my work and confidence makes me proud.

Not all images that I have created are displayed :)

Flyers

I make a lot of flyers for events that are both digital and printed. I was able to make a template that works well but sometimes the event has a special design if it usus a specific graphic.

Digital Displays

The digital displays I make also use a template that I created because of how many I have to make. It saves me a lot of time to be able to plug in the info and pictures that I format quickly. These are displayed on screens around the ISU campus, a lot of them are in the Bone Student Center.
