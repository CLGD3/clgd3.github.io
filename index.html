Work

Zine Branding

The zine branding project gave me an opportunity to think about what my interests and ideas were as a designer. It allowed me to create a branding that I would feel comfortable presenting to a client or audience. I expect it to change regularly but it's interesting to think about new ways to showcase myself in a holdable form.

Eco-Sustainability Project

In my opinion this was one of the more difficult projects I did throughout the semester. First we had to research ecological/sustainability issues that the world is currently facing, and then come up with a product that would address the issue. We had to gather data (with emphasis on local data which was the hardest) that supported the marketing of our product. We basically had to prove why the product was needed with statistics and research. Then we had to physically make our product and create branding and packaging for the prototype. My product is an easily-accessible composting bin that can be buried directly into the soil when the compost inside is ready. All materials are 100% compostable.

Social Dreaming Speculative Project

This speculative project asked the question "What would the world look like if it had only one culture?" Through research and information gathering I learned about globalization and the effects that it can have on society. While it isn't completely bad, I wanted to shed light on what could happen if we don't pay attention and steer the direction it's taking.
